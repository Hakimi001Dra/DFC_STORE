About Us

Where Craftsmanship Meets Heritage

Danfulani Collections Limited (DFC) is a dynamic and innovative fashion and lifestyle enterprise founded by Muhammad Umar Danfulani.

With over 10 years of experience in the fashion industry, DFC has grown into a reputable brand dedicated to producing high-quality fashion products and providing exceptional services to clients across Nigeria and beyond.

Founded on the principles of excellence, creativity, integrity, and customer satisfaction, DFC specializes in fashion design, tailoring, shoe production, branding solutions, interior decoration, business consulting, and talent development.

"At DFC, we believe that fashion is more than clothing—it is identity, culture, confidence, and expression."
— Muhammad Umar Danfulani, Founder DFC

Our Mission

To innovate, lead, and provide exceptional value through quality products and services while empowering talents.

Our Vision

To become a leading fashion and lifestyle brand recognized nationally and globally for innovation and quality.

Our Commitment

Delivering outstanding products, maintaining professionalism, and supporting youth empowerment.
