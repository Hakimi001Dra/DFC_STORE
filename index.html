Our Story

Danfulani Collections
Limited (DFC)

Danfulani Collections Limited (DFC) is a dynamic and innovative fashion and lifestyle enterprise founded by Muhammad Umar Danfulani — a passionate fashion designer, entrepreneur, mentor, and advocate for youth talent development with over 10 years of experience in the fashion industry.

Founded on the principles of excellence, creativity, integrity, and customer satisfaction, DFC specializes in fashion design, tailoring, shoe production, branding solutions, interior decoration, business consulting, and talent development.

Mission: To innovate, lead, and provide exceptional value through quality products and services while empowering talents, exceeding customer expectations, and contributing to economic growth.

Vision: To become a leading fashion and lifestyle brand recognized nationally and globally for innovation, quality, creativity, and talent development.

Motto: Empowering Talents, Delivering Excellence
